F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Are the rates negotiable?

    Yes they are, in fact what you have seen above is just a guide, rates will be set in a personal basis according to the student profile and location. This is to take into account the "back office" time that comes with different profile students, for instance it doesn't take us the same amount of time peparing a session for a teenager that wants to get initiated in a convenient area than some professional profile with tailor made requirements based in the outskirts of the City.

  • I am a teacher/content creator , and I want to cooperate with your project

    Those are incredible news always for us, we are looking to grow at any time, so any teacher that can cover new Geographical areas or help in something will be welcomed, please use the contact form or WhatsApp.

  • Soy hispanohablante y necesito aprender Inglés, ¿Ofreceis algún servicio al respecto?

    Por supuesto, aunque nos hayamos decantado por ofrecer clases de Español si que ofrecemos el servicio a la inversa. Nos es incluso mas sencillo para nosotros enseñar la lengua que hemos aprendido, ¡¡ Envianos un mensaje e informaté sin compromiso !!.

  • I have been using your services for some time already, but I feel like to switching to one of your new teachers as he/she look to be more related to me

    Surely , that is one of the pros of using a Tutor Aggregator Services like us, you can switch at anytime tutor, as you will be seeing new coming, maybe you got interest on certain topics that some teacher does, this is a situation when student and tutor "click". As one of the main factors for the succed on building up a new language is to get intested about the culture itself, the tutor play an important role on arising this interest , having common hobbies and the like it is such an important part.
